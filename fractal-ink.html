FractalInk Design Studio
UX Intern (2 months) • Mumbai, India
Overview

Fractal Ink Design Studio is a leading digital design studio of India, based out of Mumbai. Recently, it was acquired by Dentsu Aegis. I worked at Fractal Ink as UX designer intern in the summer of 2014.

Role and Responsibilities

I worked towards preparing User Journeys, Wireframes and Visual Design (as per guidelines) of the interface. Apart from design responsibilities, I co-ordinated with client on their requirements and their approvals on project.

Projects

I worked on Ditto TV application, product of Zee Enterprise Entertainment Limited. Ditto TV is available on all platforms (iOS, Android, Windows, Web), with 1-5 Million downloads on Play store.

Sample Screens

Within Ditto TV application, I worked majorly on 'my cirlces' section along with of 'Live TV', 'Search' and 'Playlist' modules/parts of the product. I created screens for android platform followed by for Windows platform.

The other projects allotted to me were:
Booking app for 'Book my show'
Entertainment TV application for 'Bollywood Hungama' 
Event viewing portal 'Event FAQ'.
The process that was adopted majorly comprised of brainstorming, carving out the information architecture, followed by extensive initial low-fidelity wire framing, mock-ups and analysis usability- wise. The applications' interface and functionality were designed keeping in mind the touch-based input system. Final graphical user interface was then designed. Feedback was constantly taken from the client and work was done with an iterative approach with strict deadlines.
