Design Philosophy
  • I believe in outcomes rather than output.
  • I believe in less > more
  • User experience is assimilation of product strategy, engineering and business needs.
  • I spend more time on understanding and defining problem rather than designning itself.
  • Before jumping on to design phase, I always answer Why and What
  • I beleive in going an extra mile of what is expected from you.
  • For design phase, most of my time is spent on paper rather than on any mockup tool.
  • I believe experience is not just screens and navigations, but full service including algorithms (hidden UX), engineering constraints (for eg. loading time) and business vision corresponds to end experience for users.
  • From strategy to elements on screen/microinteractions, everything has to be designed with attention to detail.
